Welcome to the Ismaili Institutions for Canada (IIC) Portal. This portal is designed to provide the Canadian Jamat with a central location to access information about the various programs and services that are available to improve the quality of our lives.

To find information by age group, simply click Audiences on the navigation bar on the top of the home page. Another easy way to find what you are looking for is to use the Search or Advanced Search box on the top right of the home page.

The A-Z Directory is also a great way to browse for information. Be sure to register for the Al-Akhbar Newsletter which is fully integrated into this portal.

For the best experience, use a modern browser such as Google Chrome, Safari, Mozilla Firefox or Internet Explorer (version 9 or better).

We hope you enjoy using the IIC Portal.
